about us

Our company was founded in 2010. Ever since, we wanted to create games taking the best from many genres, but at the same time unique in their approach.

In the cozy and beautiful city of Opole, located in the south of Poland, we try to make our dreams a reality. The silence and peace give us the proper accomodation to do our work in a truly independent style.

our city

800-years-old Opole is a city full of students, but it also offers comfort and peace, often inaccessible in bigger centres.

In this city modern architecture exists near historic buildings and landmarks, all of this available within just a few minutes walking distance.

Opole is known for its long and beautiful cycle paths, not only through the city centre, but also along the Odra river or the city's bath areas. To find nature and lots of open space, all you need to do is walk to the Bolko Island and if that's not enough, a few kilometres from Opole lies Turawa – a lake suitable for both bathing and water-based sports.
